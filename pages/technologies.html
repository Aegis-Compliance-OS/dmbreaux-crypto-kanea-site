TRIDENT

Mixed-Reality Asset Inspection & Quality Assurance

TRIDENT is KANEA's hardware and mixed-reality sensing platform, commercializing two U.S. Navy patents under a Commercial Evaluation License from the Naval Postgraduate School. TRIDENT delivers equipment digital-twin creation and mixed-reality quality assurance at the physical layer.

Where traditional inspection methods rely on paper checklists and manual documentation, TRIDENT creates living digital twins of physical assets — capturing dimensional, structural, and configuration data through MR sensing — and surfaces discrepancies in real time to the operator in the field.

STARS automatically generates compliance output from sensor data and system configuration, producing assessor-ready documentation without operator intervention.

Development

SEKON

Critical Minerals Provenance & Authentication

SEKON is KANEA's MXene sensor platform for end-to-end critical minerals authentication and supply chain provenance verification. Built on advanced sensor patents from the Air Force Research Laboratory and Army Combat Capabilities Development Command, SEKON addresses one of the most urgent blind spots in the defense industrial base.

MXene materials — two-dimensional transition metal carbides and nitrides — enable highly sensitive, selective sensing at the nanoscale. SEKON deploys this capability to verify the origin, composition, and integrity of critical mineral inputs across the defense supply chain, allied industrial partners, and regulated procurement workflows.

As with all KANEA platforms, STARS generates the provenance documentation and regulatory evidence as a native output of the sensing process — not as a downstream reporting task.
