Leadership

Dana Mincey-Breaux

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Dana Mincey-Breaux is a U.S. Army Signal Corps veteran (Captain, O-3), DoD program manager, and defense-technology entrepreneur. She founded KANEA Technology Systems to commercialize U.S. Navy patents and build secure technology infrastructure for regulated industries that have been underserved by the commercial technology market.

Her background spans active-duty Signal Corps service, Pentagon program management at the Chief Digital and Artificial Intelligence Office, and federal consulting — giving her direct experience with the compliance frameworks, acquisition pathways, and operational requirements that KANEA's platforms are built to address.

Dana holds a B.S. in Political Science from Florida A&M University, an M.A. in International Relations from Freie Universität Berlin, and a Private Equity Certificate from the Wharton School. She holds an active TS/SCI clearance and certifications including PMP, CSM, and Shipley BD & Capture.
