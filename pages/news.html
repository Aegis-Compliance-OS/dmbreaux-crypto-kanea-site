TRIDENT Demo Scheduled at TAC Defend the Airport Exercise

KANEA's TRIDENT mixed-reality inspection platform is scheduled for demonstration at the TAC Defend the Airport exercise, providing military and defense industry stakeholders a live look at the system's digital-twin and quality assurance capabilities in an operational context.

May 2026 License

NPS Commercial Evaluation License Executed for TRIDENT Patent Portfolio

KANEA Technology Systems has executed a Commercial Evaluation License (NPS-LIC-26-006) with the Naval Postgraduate School for the commercial development of U.S. Navy patents US 11,138,805 and US 11,062,523 — the foundational intellectual property underlying the TRIDENT platform.

Spring 2026 Rebrand

Aegis Compliance OS Transitions to KANEA Technology Systems

The company has rebranded from Aegis Compliance OS to KANEA Technology Systems, reflecting the strategic pivot from compliance-as-product to hardware and mixed-reality sensing platforms with compliance embedded as a native output layer. The SDVOSB/WOSB certifications and CAGE code 19Y32 are maintained through the transition.

KANEA Technology Systems will participate in the Allied Innovation Summit on K Street, Washington DC, connecting with allied-nation industrial partners, defense technology investors, and government stakeholders from Five Eyes and NATO partner nations.

In Progress Patents

SEKON Platform: MXene Sensor Patent Licensing Underway

KANEA has initiated Commercial Evaluation License applications with AFRL (US 12,054,428) and Army CCDC (US 12,132,210) for the sensor patents underlying the SEKON critical minerals provenance platform. The CEL application deadline is July 2026.
